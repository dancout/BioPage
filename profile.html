Hello!

I'm a (super :P) senior at the University of Michigan studying Computer Science Engineering. I was born in Monroe, MI, and I've lived in the Mitten ever since. I have 18 siblings, and I'd love to travel the world someday.

I became interested in computer science engineering because I love to build things. Growing up on a farm, I had access to as much lumber and as many power tools I could get my hands on. Every summer I started a different project, and constructed oddly shaped things.

Once I started college, I realized I could apply this same love for building things into a virtual world. With a computer, I can build anything I want, completely ignoring physical constraints.

I'm really interested in working in an IT Department, Web Development Department, or as a Software Engineer.
